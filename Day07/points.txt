Basic Regex
-----------
.	-	Single character (alphabetic, numbers, special characters)

^	- 	begning of the string

$	-	End of the string

*	-	zero or more occurance of a character

?	- 	zero or one occurance of character 

+	-	one or more occurance

{n}	-	exactly n times

{n,}	-	atleast n times

{n, m}  - 	min n and max m times

[]	-	character class

()	-	grouping

|	-	Alternation

\	-	escape (supress the meaning of special characters)
